ABOUT ASTROKIDS CO.

Since it has the youth culture, cartoon is the major designs of Astrokids Co. to attract the youth, and not only the youth but to everyone as well.

Astrokids Co. is offering varieties of designs especially space related objects and characters. It aims to admire the designs of "out of this world" in order to give our product a premium experience for people. And to provide people “authentic loots from outer space” which says in our tagline.
